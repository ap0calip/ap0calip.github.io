Welcome to The Numbers!

Settings:

This is a fun and educational math game made to help kids practice their arithmetic skills.

Choose your preferences:

  • Pick an operator:
    • Addition (+)
    • Subtraction (-)
    • Multiplication (×)
    • Division (÷)
  • Choose a difficulty level:
    • Easy (up to 10)
    • Medium (up to 12)
    • Hard (up to 100)
    (Visual help is available for numbers under 12.)
  • Choose a sticker style:
    • Boy
    • Girl

How to play:

  • You earn 1 star for each correct answer.
  • The maximum number of stars you can get is 8.
  • On your 9th correct answer, you win a sticker!
  • You can collect up to 20 stickers.
  • A wrong answer will take away 1 star, if you have any.

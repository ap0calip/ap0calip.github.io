The Numbers

Welcome to The Numbers! This is a fun and educational math game designed for kids to practice their arithmetic skills. Choose an operator and level to start playing!

Instructions:

  • Select an operator (+, -, ×, ÷) from the dropdown menu.
  • Choose a difficulty level (Easy 10, Medium 12, Hard 100).

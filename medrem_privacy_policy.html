Privacy Policy for MedRem

Last Updated: June 19, 2026

MedRem is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have full control over your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles your data.

1. Our Commitment to Local-Only Data

MedRem is designed as an offline-first application. We prioritize your privacy by ensuring that all data generated and managed within the app remains exclusively on your device.

2. Data We Collect and Store

We store the following information locally on your device using an embedded SQLite engine:

  • Family Member Profiles: Information used to identify individuals, including names and color tags.
  • Medication Schedules: Details regarding your medication name, dosage, instructions, and timing configurations.
  • Compliance Logs: Historical records of your intake events (e.g., "TAKEN", "SKIPPED", or "MISSED").

3. How Your Data Is Used

All data stored in the application is used solely for the purpose of:

  • Providing precise, reliable medication reminders.
  • Managing medication schedules for individuals and households.
  • Tracking compliance statistics to help you manage health routines.

4. Data Sharing and Security

  • No Data Transmission: No profile data, medication schedules, or compliance logs ever leave your device.
  • Third-Party Access: Because all data is saved locally using the Room persistence library, we do not have access to your data, and we do not share it with third parties.
  • Security: Since the application functions offline, your health information remains secure within your device’s local storage.

5. Your Controls

As the user, you maintain complete control over your data:

  • You can add, edit, or delete family member profiles.
  • You can manage, snooze, or disable medication reminders at your convenience.
  • Deleting the application will remove all local data associated with the app from your device.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy or how MedRem handles your information, please reach out through the contact channels provided in the application settings.
